Apartments

For now, the future and every need

LANDsPARK creates a perfect balance in real estate investment for both owner-occupiers and capital investors. The charm of a good old building combines with the modernity of a 2022 renovation. With the new, high-quality Berlin plank flooring, cosiness has moved in together with classic purism, which leaves plenty of scope for design. The property consists of a front building with 1-, 3- and 4-room flats and commercial units in the basement as well as the rear building located in the inner courtyard of the residential complex, which offers a 6-room maisonette flat with its own garden and further 3-room flats with balcony. High-quality basis for all, individuality for everyone: Every square metre features an upscale standard of quality without an excessive luxury investment. With new floorboards and tiles, modern bathrooms and freshly spackled walls, every unit offers the ideal basis for development. What you make out of it is entirely up to you. You can become the owner of the flats that are already rented out or those that are available immediately, move in yourself or find your own tenants in the medium term. You can create an optimal space for work and life or the family. And last but not least, you can become the owner of the undeveloped attic (loft blanks) and realise your visions from the 4th to the 6th floor.

With our show flat, we would like to demonstrate you just one of the many set ups of the flats in the garden house. The quality and equipment standard is the same across all flats, only the already installed kitchen distinguishes the show flat from the others. The two enclosed rooms can be designed as a living room, bedroom or children’s room and offer space for a generous bed, living furnishings to your taste and storage space. The passage room is ideal as a dining room for larger gatherings, as a bright and friendly workplace, as an area for fitness or other hobbies, or as a shared, open living space if the other two rooms are to be used as bedrooms. The apartments offer enough space to enfold your creativity. Whether alone, as a couple, as a family, or as a living or working community, all rooms can be designed according to your needs. The new south-facing balcony offers a view directly onto the peaceful garden. And in contrast to the typical Berlin tube bath, in this bathroom there is enough space for your own oasis of well-being.
