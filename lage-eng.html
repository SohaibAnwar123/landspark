Leave the city behind. While living in Berlin.

Our capital is known for its colourful facets. History, culture, multiculturalism, nightlife, international representatives of fashion, music or art and a rapidly
growing start-up culture unite here like in hardly any other city. Each district tells its own story and lives its own style. Friedrichshain in particular stands out
with its individuality and variety. Here you can be out and about with your family in the countryside during the day or wander through the bar and club
scene in the evening, walk in the footsteps of German history or reach concerts, sporting events and the Spree River on foot. Those who want to be part of all
this – and still a little away from the hustle and bustle – will find their home and the perfect capital investment in LANDsPARK. Opposite the property, just
across the street, is Volkspark Friedrichshain. For a relaxing walk, to sit in the greenery, to enjoy the Schönbrunn restaurant or to marvel at the open-air
cinema – the park becomes your external garden in oversize. You are also well catered for in the surrounding area, with supermarkets in all directions, the
Vivantes Clinic, a specialist medical centre, a selection of restaurants and bars without the tourist rush, tram stations as well as events at the nearby
Velodrom. If you want to use your bike instead of your feet, it takes about 10 minutes to reach the Spree, the neighbouring districts of Kreuzberg and
Prenzlauer Berg or Alexanderplatz.

Peaceful in Friedrichshain, but in the middle of Berlin – that’s LANDsPARK at Landsberger Allee 36.
