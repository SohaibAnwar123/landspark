Property in the centre of Berlin

City, Park, River

The search for the right property is all about decisions – variety or tranquility for pleasure or for the long term, right in the middle or
by the water? The LANDsPARK project at Landsberger Allee 36, right next to Volkspark Friedrichshain, is a dream home that demands no compromises. Here, urban Berlin life meets green idyll. Start-up dynamics meet a time-out atmosphere, away from the hustle and bustle and yet right in the middle of the action. The property‘s surroundings offer just as many development options as the property itself –
from 1 to 6 rooms, from a possible penthouse with a view over the rooftops of the city – to compact work-life spaces and a maisonette with
its own garden. No matter if in the front or in the rear of the property, you will find your owner-occupied apartment or investment leaves nothing to be desired. Or you can even go one better: with the existing building permit for three more storeys, you can realize your
individual visions.

LANDsPARK combines opposites. The ideal location of Landsberger Allee, close to the colourful hustle and bustle of Friedrichshain and the popular neighbouring districts of Kreuzberg, Mitte and Prenzlauer Berg, meets a green urban oasis of Volkspark Friedrichshain, is located directly across the street, the historic part of the Spree with the East Side Gallery is less than 3 kilometres to the south.
